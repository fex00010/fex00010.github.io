Website Goals

The purpose of this site is that people who crochet can easily find patterns that are great for beginners to work with, or for people of a higher skill level to whip up in no time. For the first patterns there will be 2 different hat patterns for sale. A crocheted slouchy beanie (with or without a pompom). Each pattern will be sold for 3 dollars, the pattern will include a PDF that can be used online or printed. More patterns will be added as they are created This site will also have completed projects for sale by the owner, for 15 dollars each.

About Emma-Leigh

I started Crocheting when I was 16, and I always found crochet patterns way more difficult to follow than they needed to be. So I decided to create my own patterns and share them with others that are just looking for an easy to follow crochet pattern.
